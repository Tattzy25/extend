# Embed Code

Paste this into your site where you want the TaTTTy.com AI Tattoo Image Generator to appear.

**URLs:**
- **Production:** `https://widget.tattty.com` or `https://extend-sandy.vercel.app`
- **Local dev:** `http://localhost:3000`

```html
<iframe
  src="https://widget.tattty.com"
  title="TaTTTy - AI Tattoo Image Generator"
  width="100%"
  height="900"
  style="border: none; min-height: 900px;"
  allow="clipboard-write"
  loading="lazy"
></iframe>
```

- **width="100%"** – stretches to container width
- **height="900"** – adjust for your layout (min ~700px recommended)
- **Transparent background** – embed blends with your page; ensure parent has desired background color
